What is Launchpad?

  • FlashDevelop plugin using Spaceport for iOS/Android development
  • Rapid iteration to mobile devices over Wifi
  • Run your game on iOS without paying for an apple dev account

Launchpad is a FlashDevelop plugin designed to help you get the best out of mobile Flash development. It focuses on reducing iteration times with mobile devices. It does this by allowing you to deploy your game to multiple devices over WIFI at the same time with the same FlashDevelop workflow you've gotten used to.

The greatest advantage of Launchpad is that you never have to leave your comfortable IDE environment on windows while still utilizing iOS development. With the power of Spaceport, you can develop and deploy to iOS without ever paying for an apple developer account. Thanks to the power of Spaceport, developing on iOS without ever touching troublesome iOS provisioning profiles or certificates. All you have to do is push your game over Wifi to a do-nothing free app that you can easily get on the appstore.

Check out Spaceport here, http://spaceport.io where you can download and try out their command line tools.
